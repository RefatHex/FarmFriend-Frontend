Innovation in Farming

Farm-Friend provides crop recommendations based on past data and farming techniques. It sends real-time weather alerts to help farmers plan their activities and avoid losses. Farmers can also use our platform to rent or share farming tools, saving money and resources.

The system further suggests fertilizers based on soil tests, ensuring crops get the nutrients they need. It provides up-to-date market prices while connecting farmers, enabling them to communicate and sell crops at the best rates. Additionally, it connects farmers directly to storage owners, simplifying the process of storing and selling harvests without middlemen.
